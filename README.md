Integrated Healthcare Analytics Dashboard
Project Story:

Overview:
Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of patient, clinical, financial, and operational data. However, converting this data into actionable insights remains a challenge.
To address this, I developed an Integrated Healthcare Analytics Dashboard using Power BI, Power Query, and DAX that transforms raw healthcare data into meaningful business intelligence across five analytical layers.

Executive Summary(page-1)
Designed an executive-level overview to monitor key performance indicators including Total Patients, Total Billing, Average Billing, and Average Length of Stay, enabling quick assessment of overall hospital performance.

Patient Demographics & Health Profile Analysis(page-2)
Analyzed patient distribution across age groups, gender, blood groups, and medical conditions to identify population trends and high-risk patient segments.

Clinical Insights & Treatment Analysis(page-3)
Evaluated disease distribution, diagnostic test results, and medication usage to uncover clinical patterns and support healthcare decision making.

Financial & Insurance Performance Analysis(page-4)
Investigated revenue generation across medical conditions and insurance providers while tracking billing trends to identify key revenue drivers and financial opportunities.

Operational Insights & Strategic Recommendations(page-5)
Examined resource utilization and patient stay patterns to uncover operational efficiencies and provide data-driven recommendations for improving patient outcomes and hospital profitability.

KEY INSIGHTS:
1.Analyzed over 55K patient records.
2.Total billing exceeded 1.42 Billion.
3.Diabetes generated the highest overall revenue.
4.Obesity recorded the highest average billing per patient.
5.Average patient length of stay was 15.51 days.

KEY INNOVATIONS:
1.Built a complete end-to-end healthcare analytics solution covering patient, clinical, financial, and operational domains.
2.Developed custom DAX measures and KPI-driven dashboards for executive decision-making.
3.Combined healthcare outcome analysis with financial performance evaluation to provide a holistic view of hospital operations.
4.Derived strategic recommendations based on analytical findings rather than presenting descriptive visualizations alone.
5.Designed a multi-page storytelling dashboard that guides users from high-level KPIs to actionable business insights.
